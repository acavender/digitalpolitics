Digital Politics

About

This is a sample website for Digital Politics, a new course at Saint Mary's College, offered for the first time in the Spring 2021 semester.

The course explores the intersections of politics and the digital world. We'll explore such questions as: How does the digital world — including social media — impact elections? Do digital technologies negatively impact any of our constitutional rights? How do they impact privacy? Do they have any implications for the way(s) we think about bias, equality, and access?
